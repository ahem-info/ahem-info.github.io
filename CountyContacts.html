AHEM Contacts

AHEM Contacts provide information and support, helping with questions about the Charles guidelines and acting as a sounding board for concerns in dealing with school officials. AHEM's resources allow Contacts to have a broad overview and solid understanding of how homeschooling works in Massachusetts as well as an awareness of hot spots and trends.
