Not-Back-to-School Events - 2013

Friday, September 6, 2013, 10:00 am

(Rain Date- Monday September 9 same time)
South Shore Home Learners NOT Back to School Beach Day and Picnic, Duxbury Beach (access via Powder Point Bridge)

Join South Shore Home Learners for a Not Back to School Beach Day & Picnic! Meet other homeschooling families, make new friends and connections! If you're new to homeschooling, bring any questions you have. Look for a South Shore Home Learners T-shirt displayed near our group. We'll probably be the only group with school-aged kids :-)

Parking: After Labor Day, non-residents and cars without stickers are allowed to park in the lot to the left at the end of the Powder Point Bridge. Last year there was some confusion, as there was a sign saying that you needed a sticker. Just tell the guard that you are meeting friends and that since it's after Labor Day, you know a sticker is not required. You can also print out and refer to the parking regulations (Motor Vehicle Beach Regulations #2): http://www.town.duxbury.ma.us/Public_Documents/DuxburyMA_BTPermits/welcome.pdf

If the weather looks iffy, we will post any change in plans by 8 a.m. on 9/6. Questions: Email heidiconner at hotmail dot com.

Friday, September 6, 2013, 11:00 am - 5:00 pm

Homeschooling Together's Not Back To School Picnic at Walden Pond, Route 126, Concord, MA.

After everyone else has gone back to school spend a day at the beach with other homeschoolers. The fun starts at 11:00. Bring a picnic lunch and meet at Walden Pond, main beach. Contact 781.646.4541. Rain date: Friday, September 13.

Wednesday, September 11, 2013, 12:30-3:30 pm

Please join us for a Not Back to School picnic at the playground at Larz Anderson Park in Brookline. The parking lot for the playground is off Goddard St, near the baseball diamonds, not the one near the marker on the map. We hope to see old friends and make new ones! Rain date Sept 18.

Friday, September 13, 2013, 11:00 am- 2:00 pm

Join us on Friday the 13th for a Not Back to School Picnic at Stage Fort Park in Gloucester, MA. Any day is lucky when you're with a group of homeschool friends! Feel free to bring labeled foods to share, but please no items with peanuts to protect the health of one of our seriously allergic children.

Monday, September 16, 2013, 12:30 PM - 5:00 PM

The Nuts About Learning group is having a Not-Back-To-School Picnic at Stage Fort Park in Gloucester, MA. Contact Molly, mbelisle2 at hotmail dot com.
