AHEM Needs YOU!

Your skills, expertise, and helping hands!

AHEM is staffed by a handful of currently homeschooling or formerly homeschooling parent volunteers. To stay viable, we need young families with skin in the game who care about keeping independent homeschooling as an educational option to step up. Help out in order for Massachusetts homeschoolers to keep the right to homeschool the way they want to homeschool.

Several opportunities are available:

  • Graphic Design We often supply materials like brochures to libraries or hand them out at presentations and we'd like to update our designs.
  • Help Plan Events We would love to resume more in-person or virtual events but need logistical help to make this happen.
  • Town Point Person Town point people are independent, local volunteers who help others find social connection and opportunities in their town. More info here.
  • Write Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers Piece Would you like to share your homeschooling experience? We are seeking pieces for our Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers feature. This is a great chance to talk about yourself, what makes your homeschooling unique. We are not ageist and welcome writing by children, teens, and adults. More info here.
  • Train to Provide One-on-One Help Are you an experienced homeschooler looking to help others? Our one-on-one contacts respond to inquiries from homeschoolers across the state. We will train and equip you to help with this important part of our mission. See our volunteer application.

If you are interested in any of these opportunities, or have another skill or talent you think would be valuable to share, please get in touch or fill out our volunteer application today. We are more than happy to give you details.

Thanks so much for considering!
