Homeschooling Events at Local Libraries

We recently sent out a survey to librarians across Massachusetts to see how their libraries serve the homeschooling community and what events they offer for homeschoolers. If your local library does not yet offer events and services for homeschoolers, please consider reaching out to point them to the AHEM website and to have them complete our recent survey for librarians so that we can follow up with them.

Jamaica Plain

Park day; potential co-op in Boston area

Medway

Homeschool Hangout Mondays at 1 (social/unstructured art time)

Methuen

Homeschool Hub for ages 5-10; past topics have included slime, make your own ice cream (May), the solar system, Ancient Egypt, monarch butterflies, worldwide wintertime celebrations, and more!

Millbury

Homeschool Mini Conference June 11th, including a workshop from AHEM and a curriculum swap.

North Chelmsford (MacKay Library)

Homeschool hangout - Drop in to play some board games, hang out, and meet other homeschooling families! Also, get to know one of our Youth Services librarians, Ms. Stephanie, and let her know if you need any help with library programs or resources! Open to all ages!

Quincy

Join us on the 2nd Friday of each month (September-May) for a homeschool program for kids from 5-13 and their caregivers, covering topics from STEM to art and beyond!

Raynham

Homeschool Crafternoon: Monthly craft projects for ages 5-12.

Westminster

Homeschool Showcase & Curriculum Swap on 5/22 at 3:30 PM

Weymouth

Curtain Call Book Club-Homeschool Edition
