The Year in Review 2024

As another year draws to a close, we at AHEM find ourselves grateful for the opportunity to walk with our own children through these formative years; for having additional time to listen and reflect on life; to learn and grow as families and a homeschooling community. AHEM is driven by the goal of supporting your family to nurture, learn with and support your children during these special years. It is a privilege to walk this path with you and we are grateful for your engagement with us!

At AHEM, we are committed to guiding homeschoolers and those exploring homeschooling to become their own best advocates. We pride ourselves on providing up-to-date information and comprehensive explanations about homeschooling requirements in Massachusetts. This dedication is reflected in our 5-star ratings from follow-up surveys conducted after our one-on-one consultations.

Our volunteers work diligently to ensure every inquiry is addressed within 24 hours, offering answers and the reassurance our community needs to proceed with confidence. As a fully volunteer-run nonprofit, we rely on the generosity of donors to sustain our essential programs and services.

Your support is crucial in helping us continue our mission of empowering individuals and fostering connections within our community. Join us in making a difference—every contribution counts!

Please consider AHEM when you are making end-of-year charitable donations!

Volunteers

Volunteers are at the heart of what we are able to do! If you are interested in joining them, fill out the application here.

Ongoing volunteer opportunities include:
