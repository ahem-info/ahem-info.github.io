Announcing Town Point People

AHEM is building a list of town or school district-specific point people to help homeschooling families connect families with others in town. So far we have a Town Point Person for:

  • Arlington
  • Beverly
  • Boston
  • Dedham
  • Dighton/Rehoboth
  • Dracut
  • Framingham
  • Hamilton/Wenham
  • Hampden 
  • Kingston
  • Lexington
  • Longmeadow 
  • Mansfield
  • Marion
  • Mattapoisett
  • Millbury
  • Milton 
  • Newton
  • Norton
  • Plainville
  • Rehoboth
  • Rochester 
  • Salem
  • Seekonk
  • Swampscott
  • Wenham
  • Wilbraham 
  • Wilmington
  • Winthrop 

These volunteers are eager to meet others in town. Get in touch if you want to be connected to the Town Point Person in your town, or find out more about being a Town Point Person yourself! In either case, you must be an active subscriber to AHEM emails.
