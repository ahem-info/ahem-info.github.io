AHEM Networking Forum

In an attempt to harness the power of technology in service of connection, the AHEM Networking Forum was created to facilitate families in different parts of Massachusetts to meet in person and get to know each other.

Since the forum began in 2019, Google has changed the way groups work, so we have recently updated the forum guidelines to make the forum more straightforward to join and use.

To read more about how it works, and to potentially join, go take a look!
