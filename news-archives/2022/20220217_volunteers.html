AHEM Needs You!

Your skills, expertise, and helping hands!

AHEM is “staffed” by a small handful of currently homeschooling or formerly homeschooling parent volunteers. To stay viable, we need young families with skin in the game who care about keeping independent homeschooling as an educational option to step up and help out if Massachusetts homeschoolers are to keep the right to homeschool the way they want to homeschool.

Several virtual opportunities are available:

    Town Point Person - As a town point person you would be an entry point for homeschooling families, new and established, with others in your town trying to connect with others locally. More info here.
    • Write Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers piece - Do you have a unique homeschooling experience? We are seeking pieces for our Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers feature. This is a great chance to talk about yourself, what makes your homeschooling unique. We are not ageist and welcome writing by children, teens, and adults. More info here.
  • Help Monitor Legislation - Assist our board member in keeping an eye on state-wide legislation that may affect homeschooling. Complete volunteer application here.

If you are interested in any of these opportunities, or have another skill or talent you think would be valuable to share, please get in touch! We are more than happy to give you details. Thanks so much for considering!
