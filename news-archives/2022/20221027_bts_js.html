Behind the Scenes at AHEM: Jeff Sullivan

Jeff Sullivan lives in Dedham with his family (including three children). When not building healthcare data pipelines, helping AHEM use their survey data to answer important questions about home education in Massachusetts, or discussing history and engineering with his kids, Jeff spends his time playing Dungeons & Dragons and complicated board games with friends and family, reading science fiction, and having coffee at Mocha Java.
