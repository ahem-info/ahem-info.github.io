Volunteer Opportunities

Superintendent mailing

Tuesday, March 31, 2 – 4 pm, Arlington, MA

We need help folding and stuffing envelopes. We also welcome help in the form of bringing snacks and drinks to share, and quiet activities (games, crafts) for kids to play alongside. Interested in helping out? Email info@ahem.info and let us know what you’d like to contribute.

Massachusetts Library Association (MLA) Conference tabling

May 18 and 19, 2020 in Hyannis, MA

An AHEM representative will be at the table at all times, but we'd love some company. Are you interested in doing a shift? Let us know your availability for a 2-hour shift  May 18, 10:00 am – 5:00 pm or May 19, 8:00 am – 3:30 pm. (Sorry - kids are not allowed.)

SPED consultant

Do you have expertise or experience in navigating homeschooling and special needs? Would you be willing to consult with AHEM when we get a question? We would love to chat!

Lawyer

Are you a lawyer with knowledge and experience about Massachusetts homeschool law? Would you be willing to consult with AHEM when we need a legal opinion? Let’s get to know each other!

Library Survey

Got 5 minutes? Help us prepare for the MLA conference by responding to this questionnaire about homeschoolers and libraries by April 15, 2020.

Website proofreading

Read and operate features on the Advocates for Home Education in Massachusetts, Inc.’s website. Advise the webmaster of misspellings, broken links, confusing wording or navigation problems. Email to get started.

Write a Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers piece

If you've always wanted to write and get published, this is the opportunity for you. We are seeking pieces to consider for our Meet Massachusetts Homeschoolers feature which highlights the diversity of Massachusetts homeschoolers. This is a great chance to talk about yourself and what makes your homeschooling unique. We welcome writing by children, teens, and adults. Email for more info.
