Student Charlie Cards

The MBTA issues student fare media to middle school and high school aged homeschoolers for use on the MBTA system. In August, they will be issuing student Charlie Cards for the 2020-2021 Academic School Year.

Please complete the order request form.

A homeschool approval/acknowledgement letter from your Superintendent’s office or town is required. An acceptable alternative to such a letter would be a copy of a membership card from a recognized homeschoolers' organization.

Per the MBTA: Any advocacy for homeschoolers, homeschoolers support groups, and/or homeschoolers organizations that we can verify will be considered a recognized homeschoolers organization. An AHEM membership card should work.

All homeschool monthly passes must be paid for in advance.

All student CharlieCards (M7s and S-cards) expire on August 31st each year.

Student tickets are for students in Middle or High School only. Children under the age of twelve ride for free when accompanied by a paying adult. No ID required.

See details about MBTA reduced fares here .

Summer Monthly Paper Passes M7

Summer Monthly Paper Passes M7, good for July and/or August only, can be ordered using this form.

The deadline for requesting summer passes is July 1, 2020.

AHEM clarified with the MBTA how the following rules stated on the form intersect with homeschoolers buying M7 cards:

* Company or Organization checks ONLY

* NO individual checks/money-orders collected from students and/or parents will be accepted

The Student Pass program responded that Accounts Payable is trying to eliminate individual payments for summer passes for one invoice. Thus if a personal check must be submitted, it must only be one per order form and not 10 or 20 for one order.

So: one family per order form, submitted to the MBTA with a single check.

All student CharlieCards (M7s and S-cards) expire on August 31st each year.
