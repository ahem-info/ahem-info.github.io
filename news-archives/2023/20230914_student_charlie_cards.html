Student Charlie Cards 2023-2024

The MBTA issues reduced-rate Student Charlie Cards to middle school and high school aged homeschoolers for use on the MBTA system.

A homeschool approval/acknowledgement letter from your superintendent’s office or town is required. Alternatives to an approval letter will be considered on a case-by-case basis.

To request passes, please complete the order request form.

For more details about this program, click here.

Children 11 years old and younger ride for free when accompanied by a paying adult. No ID required.
