Vendor's Invitation to the AHEM Home Learners' Fall Fair - Watertown

The AHEM Home Learners Fall Fair will be held on Saturday, November 3, 1:30 - 4:30 pm at First Parish of Watertown, 35 Church St., Watertown, MA. We are planning this to be a festive event with crafts for sale, entertainment, and refreshments. Homeschooling families are invited to display and sell their crafts, or perform. This is a wonderful opportunity for children and families to learn entrepreneurial skills and experience a larger sense of community.  We hope you will join us. Deadline for submitting vendor applications is October 6.

The fair is intended for homemade, homegrown items. Examples from past fairs include jewelry, homemade soaps, candles, pet toys, sewn and knitted items, photography, etc. The fair is not a venue for small businesses to sell their wares. If you’re in doubt as to whether your product qualifies, just ask! Vendors may not sell bake sale goods or foods meant to be bought and consumed on the premises.

A note to parents: Children can be disheartened if their crafts don’t sell up to their expectations.  Prepare your children for this possibility. Use it to discuss marketing, demand, pricing, or other factors that will influence sales. Call this an experiment to test the market and learn. Most customers are other homeschooling families, so encourage friends and family to come and shop!

We are looking for performers. Performers are an integral part of this event. People come for the performances as much as for the crafts, and more attendees benefit all. Would you like to perform? Are you part of a performing group?  Would you or some of your group be interested in performing at the Fair? Contact info@ahem.info.

Where: First Parish of Watertown, 35 Church St., Watertown, MA
When: Saturday, November 3, 2018, 1:30 PM – 4:30 PM
Vendor Cost: 3 ft. @ $20 + $2.03 Eventbrite fee

(The number of electrical outlets is limited and we cannot guarantee access to one.)

Click here for the vendor application. Deadline for submitting applications is October 6. Questions should be directed to info@ahem.info.

Participants will be added to the Friends of AHEM list unless they specifically opt out.

Proceeds, if any, will be donated to support the non-profit mission of AHEM, a 501(c)(3) charitable organization.

The planners and sponsors of this event reserve the right to cancel this function without penalty. The total amount of liability of the planners and sponsors will be limited to a refund of the registration fee.
