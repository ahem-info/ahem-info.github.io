Association of HomeSchool Attorneys

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/AHSA-USA/
This list is an opportunity for homeschoolers to contact homeschooling attorneys and experts about homeschooling legal and litigation issues. It is an informal network of attorneys and legal experts that are concerned with litigation pending and threatened against homeschoolers. Its primary purpose is to exchange legal information within the profession, and to educate and support attorneys and experts involved in homeschool litigation. Find Your Elected Officials

Find election information for all levels of government by entering your address: http://www.wheredoivotema.com/bal/myelectioninfo.aspx.

You will probably be able to find names of elected officials on a town's home page. Of special interest are school committee members, as well as information about when the school committee meets, and if it is possible to receive advance notice of their agenda in case the topic of homeschooling comes up.

If you can't find your town's website, try calling your city or town clerk for the information. You will find the phone number here  http://www.sec.state.ma.us/cis/cistel/telidx.htm.
