Sample Progress Report

Here's a sample progress report that has been accepted in Massachusetts. Your school district may request more "evidence of progress" i.e. specific examples of progress in each subject over the year. If they do, you can resubmit the report adding info to it. But if you are expected to submit evaluation, it can't hurt to start with what is minimally acceptable and go from there.

[Personalize with your own subjects]

In the areas of hours of instruction, variety of educational materials and resources, and learning in specific subject areas, CHILD has met or exceeded the goals we laid out in our educational plan.

Reading/Writing/Literature: has shown significant progress

Mathematics: has shown significant progress

Social Studies/History: has shown significant progress

Science: has shown significant progress

The Arts: has shown significant progress

Physical Education: has shown significant progress
